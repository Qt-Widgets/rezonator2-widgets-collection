Sagittal Cylindrical Thin Lens
==============================

The sagittal cylindrical lens acts just like a general :doc:`thin lens <ElemThinLens>` but transforms a beam only in the :ref:`sagittal plane <working_planes>`. In the other plane, it has a unity matrix and does not affect the beam.
