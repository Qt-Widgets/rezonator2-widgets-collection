Change waist $w_0$

Lock waist

Calculate the beam radius $w$ and the wavefront ROC $R$ at given distance $z$. $$ z_0 = \frac{\pi w_0^2}{M^2 \lambda} $$ $$ V_s = \frac{M^2 \lambda}{\pi w_0} $$ $$ w = w_0 \sqrt{ 1 + \bigg( \frac{z}{z_0} \bigg)^2 } $$ $$ R = z \Bigg[ 1 + \bigg( \frac{z_0}{z} \bigg)^2 \Bigg] $$ $$ q^{-1} = \frac 1 R + i \frac{\lambda}{\pi w^2} $$

Lock front

For specified waist radius $w_0$, find such a distance $z$ and beam quality $M^2$ at which the wavefront ROC keeps its previously calculated value. How to find: express $z_0$ from $w(z)$ and from $R(z)$, equate both expressions and solve against $z$. $$ z = R \Bigg[ 1 - \bigg( \frac{w_0}{w} \bigg)^2 \Bigg] $$ $$ z_0 = \sqrt{ \frac{ z^2 w_0^2 }{ w^2 - w_0^2 } } $$ $$ z_0 = \sqrt{ z (R - z) } $$ $$ M^2 = \frac{ \pi w_0^2 }{ \lambda z_0 } $$ $$ V_s = \frac{M^2 \lambda}{\pi w_0} $$
